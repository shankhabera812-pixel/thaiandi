Our Story

A Taste of Thailand
in the Heart of
West Boylston

At Brown Rice 2, every dish is made from scratch using authentic Thai recipes and the freshest ingredients. We believe real Thai cooking isn't about shortcuts — it's about balance: the heat of chilies, the brightness of lemongrass, and the comfort of slow-simmered curry.

From our hand-ground curry pastes to our house-made sauces, we pour our heritage into every plate. Come as you are — whether it's a quiet lunch for two or a lively family dinner, you'll always leave full and happy.
