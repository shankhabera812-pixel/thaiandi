Our Story

A Taste of Thailand
in the Heart of Shrewsbury

Thai & I was founded by Fawn Weydt alongside her mother, Pat Rakkun — a culinary teacher from Songkhla in southern Thailand. Pat created the recipes that anchor our menu, and her heritage shapes every dish we serve. This is a family-owned restaurant, run with the same care you’d find at a home kitchen in Thailand.

Our chefs trained at New York City restaurants before bringing their craft to Shrewsbury. Every dish is made fresh daily. The dining room is warm and cozy — about 22 seats — with an intimate atmosphere that invites you to linger. We don’t serve alcohol, but you’re welcome to bring your own. Find us near Route 9, across from CVS.
