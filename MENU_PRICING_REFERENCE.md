## QUICK N' EASY SETS (All Day)

### How Sets Work
Each set is a **complete meal** at a flat price that includes:
1. **One entrée** with your protein choice (upcharges apply — see below)
2. **One appetizer** (choice of): Chicken Crispy Roll (2 Pcs) · Veggie Crispy Roll (2 Pcs) · Crab Rangoon (2 Pcs) · Tofu Triangles (4 Pcs) · Fried Veggie Dumplings (2 Pcs)
3. **Jasmine rice** (implied by set structure)
4. **Spicy level** selection (1★ – 5★, no spicy = default)

## NOTES FOR MENU.JSON BUILD
- **Every section has its own protein price table.** Do not copy one section's prices to another.
- Lunch Noodles vs Lunch Combinations vs Lunch Curry all differ — verified separately.
- Dinner Noodles vs Dinner Combinations vs Dinner Curry all differ — verified separately.
- Duck ($27.95) is consistent across all dinner sections as the premium protein.
- Crispy Chicken pricing varies by section: Lunch Noodles $17.95 · Lunch Combinations $14.95 · Dinner Noodles $17.95 · Dinner Combinations $17.95 · Dinner Curry $19.95.
- Curry types (both lunch and dinner): Red · Yellow · Green · Panang · Massaman — these are modifier choices within the single "Lunch Curry" / "Curry (Dinner)" item on Toast.
- The Set appetizer choice is fixed to 5 options; include this in set descriptions.
- Items with no base price ($0.00 on Toast) require protein selection before add-to-cart.
